## What to Look For When Choosing a Software Partner in Nepal

When evaluating **software companies in Nepal** for your next project, consider these factors:

- **Technical expertise:** Does the company have proven experience with your tech stack? Nepal has strong .NET and JavaScript ecosystems — choose a partner whose skills align with your project.
- **Communication:** English proficiency is generally excellent among Nepali developers, but verify that communication protocols (daily standups, Slack, project management tools) match your expectations.
- **Time zone overlap:** Nepal Standard Time (UTC+5:45) offers 5+ hours of overlap with US East Coast and full overlap with Europe. Make sure your working hours align.
- **Retention and stability:** Ask about team retention rates. Companies like Ojas Technologies boast 95%+ retention, which means your team stays consistent.
- **Security and compliance:** If you're in healthcare, finance, or legal, ensure the company understands HIPAA, SOC 2, GDPR, or other relevant frameworks.
- **Past work and references:** Review their portfolio and speak with past clients. Most reputable firms will share case studies and references.
