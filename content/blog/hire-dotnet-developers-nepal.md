## The Rise of Nepal as a Tech Outsourcing Destination

Nepal's software development ecosystem has matured significantly over the past decade. With a growing pool of English-speaking engineers, strong time zone overlap with US and European clients, and competitive rates, Nepal is emerging as a preferred destination for IT outstaffing.

Here's why leading companies are choosing Nepal for their .NET development needs.

## 1. Strong Time Zone Alignment

Nepal Standard Time (UTC+5:45) overlaps significantly with:

- **US Eastern:** 9:45 AM – 12:15 AM (strong morning overlap)
- **Europe:** 3:15 PM – 5:45 PM (full afternoon overlap)
- **UK:** 2:15 PM – 4:45 PM (full afternoon overlap)

This means your Nepal-based developers work during your business hours — no overnight shifts or delayed communication.
